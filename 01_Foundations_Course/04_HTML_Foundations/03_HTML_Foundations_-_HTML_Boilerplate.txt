What is the purpose of the doctype declaration?
To determine that the document is HTML and it follows HTML5 conventions.

What is the HTML element? 
The HTML element is the root element and every other element is a descendant.

What is the purpose of the head element?
The head element is where we put metadata about our website and NOT any content.

What is the purpose of the body element?
The body element is where the content of the website goes that users can see.
